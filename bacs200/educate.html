Inside the Mind of a Procrastinator - Tim Urban

Tim Urban, author of the blog, "Wait But Why" talks about procrastination as a procrastinator himself. He
explains the issues with instant gratification and the "dark playground" where people go to procrastinate before
big dealines. Tim takes the complex issues of procrastinator in a funny poignant way that connects with viewers.

How to Speak so People Want to Listen - Julian Treasure

Julian Treasure a top rated international speaker on sound and communication skills talks about how to get
people to want to listen to you. He talks about the golden rules of speaking:Honesty, Authenticity, Integrity,
and Love.

How to Spot a Liar - Pamela Meyer

Pamela Meyer, Author of "Liespotting: Proven Techniques to detect Deception," Talks about the cooperative
action of lying. Dr. Meyer goes on to explain how inhert lying is in our society and then goes on to teach
watchers how to spot a lair.

RSA Animate: The Paradox of Choice

This video explores the paradox of choice. If we are given no choice we are unhappy, but if we are
given too many choices we will never be happy with what we choose. Choice is anxiety promoting
for several reasons:

  • Choice is a social Matter
  • We try to make an ideal choice
  • Choice always involves a loss

The Truth About Dishonesty

This video explores dishonestly, how most people are not big cheaters, but most people are little cheaters.

cheated at all.
