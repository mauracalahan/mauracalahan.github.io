About

Foothills animal Shelter is an open admission shelter that cares for thousands for cats, kittens, dogs, puppies, and critters every year with staff and the help of volunteers. They offer services ranging from pet adoption, Jefferson County pet licensing, affordable spaying and neutering, vaccinations, microchipping and lost and found pets. Foothills Animal Shelter is committed to the Socially Conscious Animal Community movement. The goal of SCAC movement is to create the best outcomes for all animals.

Mission Statement

Foothills Animal Shelter is dedicated to providing support to our surrounding community through education, services that strengthen the human-pet bond, and humane care for the animals we serve.
